### Post 2 — AI and Bias in Performance Reviews
Most performance management tools help you run reviews faster.

Few help you run them more fairly.

There's a difference.

"Runs fast" means your HR team spends less time chasing submissions.
"More fairly" means a woman in engineering doesn't get rated lower because her manager described her as "assertive" where they'd have written "strong leader" for a male colleague.

That second one has legal implications. Financial ones too.

We built TalentPulse to solve the second problem — using GPT-4o to detect bias patterns in review text before they're submitted.

### Post 3 — Team Health Score
Your team's health isn't one number.

But if you had to summarize it in one number, what would you use?

At TalentPulse, our Team Health Score is a composite of three signals:
1. **Engagement** — Are employees completing reviews and giving feedback?
2. **OKR Attainment** — What % of objectives are on track vs. at risk?
3. **Feedback Sentiment** — Is peer feedback trending positive or negative?

Each pillar gets a score (0–100). They're averaged into one number you can track week over week.

When the score drops, you know where to look.

When it rises, you know what's working.

Simple? Yes. But surprisingly useful.
