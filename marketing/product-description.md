# TalentPulse — AI-Powered Performance Analytics

> **"Performance management that runs on data, not gut feel."**

---

## What Is TalentPulse?

TalentPulse is a modern, AI-powered performance management platform that helps HR leaders and managers run fair, data-driven performance reviews, track team OKRs, and develop employees with personalized AI coaching — all in one place.

---

## The Problem

Traditional performance management is broken:

- **Biased reviews** — unconscious bias based on gender, race, and age affects how employees are evaluated
- **Infrequent feedback** — annual reviews are too late to change anything
- **Subjective scoring** — managers rate differently, creating inconsistent outcomes
- **No actionable insights** — reviews happen, get filed away, and nothing changes
- **Manual OKR tracking** — spreadsheets and stale slides instead of real-time visibility

The result: good employees leave, managers waste hours on paperwork, and companies make talent decisions on gut feel.

---

## The Solution

TalentPulse automates the entire performance management lifecycle with AI:

1. **Write reviews** → AI detects and flags bias in real time
2. **Track OKRs** → Real-time dashboards, no spreadsheets
3. **Collect 360 feedback** → Structured, anonymous, sentiment-analyzed
4. **Generate coaching plans** → GPT-4o creates personalized development suggestions
5. **Measure team health** → One score that aggregates engagement, OKR attainment, and feedback sentiment

---

## Core Features

### 🤖 AI Bias Detection
Every performance review is automatically scanned by GPT-4o for gender, racial, age, and other biases. Flagged language is highlighted before submission — reducing legal risk and improving fairness across the board.

### 🎯 OKR Tracking
Set and track Objectives and Key Results at the individual and team level. Real-time progress bars, status badges (On Track / At Risk / Completed), and quarterly rollups make alignment visible at a glance.

### 💬 360° Peer Feedback
Collect structured, anonymous peer feedback at any time — not just during review cycles. Every submission is sentiment-analyzed (positive / neutral / negative) to surface trends managers can act on.

### 🧠 AI Coaching Suggestions
After each review cycle, GPT-4o generates 3–5 personalized development suggestions for each employee — based on their actual reviews, OKR progress, and peer feedback. Managers start 1:1s with a plan, not a blank page.

### 📊 Team Health Score
A single composite score (0–100) that combines engagement signals, OKR attainment rates, and feedback sentiment. Updated weekly. Drill down to see which pillar needs attention.

### 📈 Analytics Dashboard
Executive-level charts and breakdowns: review completion rates, OKR attainment by department, sentiment trends over time, and team health trajectory.

### 💳 Stripe Billing
Self-serve plans from Starter to Enterprise. Upgrade, downgrade, and manage billing directly in-app with Stripe Customer Portal.
